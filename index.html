About Our Institute

Providing quality computer education with experienced faculty and modern facilities

Welcome to Guru Computer Education Institute

Established in 2015, Guru Computer Education Institute has been a pioneer in providing quality computer education in Bilha, Bilaspur. Our mission is to empower students with practical computer skills that are essential in today's digital world.

We offer a wide range of courses from basic computer literacy to advanced professional courses like Tally, DCA, PGDCA, and AI tools including ChatGPT.
